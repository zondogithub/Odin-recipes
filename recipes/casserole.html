Chicken Enchilada Casserole

A very easy, inexpensive dish made with just 4 affordable ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Enchilada sacuce
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Montenery Jack Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breast into 3 pieces put it in a medium saucepan. Pour the enchilada sauce over it and cook for 20 minutes over medium heat.
  2. Preheat the oven at 375 F.Remove chicken from the heat once cooked and shred it.
  3. Spray the baking dish with cooking spray and pur the leftover enchilada sauce. Next, place the corn tortillas on the bottom followed by a third of the shredded chicken and cheese. Make another layer and repeat the process.
  4. Transfer the casserole to the preheated oven and bake it uncovered for 25 minutes. Serve it warm with sour cream, avocado or salsa if you like!
