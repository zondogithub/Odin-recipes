Egg And Mayo Sandwich

A very easy and affordable lunch meal with only three ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of brown bread
  • 8 large eggs
  • cup of mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Boil the eggs for 7 minutes.
  2. Once cooked, teake them off the heat and peel them.
  3. Cutethem into small chunks and add in the mayonnaise and mix untill it is all combined well.
  4. Apply the egg and mayonnaise mixture like a spread on all the bread slices and enjoy.
